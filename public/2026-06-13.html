RNase III-deficient Bacillus thuringiensis enables stable dsRNA production and enhances HD1 activity against Spodoptera frugiperda.

RNase III缺陷型苏云金芽孢杆菌实现稳定dsRNA生产并增强HD1对草地贪夜蛾的杀虫活性

Zhu Y, Cao D, Guo T, Zhang X, Luo Y, Li X, Yang H, Zhang M

酶/系统类型: Class I RNase III（由rnc基因编码的内源性RNase III）
机制要点: 通过CRISPR-Cas9敲除苏云金芽孢杆菌中的rnc基因，消除内源性RNase III对双链RNA（dsRNA）的降解活性，从而显著提高细胞内dsRNA的积累稳定性。发夹结构dsRNA（hpdsRNA）在DBΔrnc菌株中转录水平高于双启动子构建体，且口服递送靶向草地贪夜蛾内切几丁质酶基因SfCHI的hpdsRNA可有效诱导RNA干扰，导致蛹化缺陷。
工程化与应用: 利用优化的CRISPR-Cas9系统构建RNase III缺陷型菌株DBΔrnc作为dsRNA表达底盘；通过发夹dsRNA设计优化转录效率；将DBΔrnc表达的dsRNA与Bt HD1菌株联合应用，实现RNAi与Bt生物防治的整合，增强对草地贪夜蛾的杀虫活性。
关键结果: DBΔrnc菌株中dsRNA积累量显著高于野生型；口服递送DBΔrnc表达的hpdsCHI导致草地贪夜蛾蛹化缺陷；DBΔrnc(hpdsCHI)与HD1联合应用显著提高初孵和2龄幼虫死亡率，证实了RNAi与Bt协同防治的可行性。

Spodoptera frugiperda is one of the most important invasive agricultural pests in China. Integrating RNA interference (RNAi) with Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt)-based biocontrol represents a promising strategy for pest control. However, efficient dsRNA production in Bt remains challenging as endogenous RNase activity limits dsRNA stability and accumulation. In this study, an RNase III-deficient Bt strain, DBΔrnc, was generated using an optimized CRISPR-Cas9 system. Deficiency of RNase III markedly enhanced intracellular dsRNA accumulation. A hairpin dsRNA construct exhibited higher transcript levels than the dual-promoter construct, especially in the DBΔrnc background. Oral delivery of Bt expressed hairpin dsRNA targeting the endochitinase gene SfCHI of S. frugiperda induced effective gene silencing and caused pupation defects.
